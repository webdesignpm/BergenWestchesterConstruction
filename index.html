About Bergen Westchester

Three Decades of Construction Excellence

Founded in 1999 by Michael Venezia, Bergen Westchester Construction LLC has been transforming homes and commercial spaces across New Jersey and New York for over three decades. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted choice for discerning property owners.

We pride ourselves on delivering projects on time, within budget, and exceeding expectations. Every project comes with our comprehensive 5-year warranty and satisfaction guarantee.

Our Process

From Vision to Reality

Our proven 4-step process ensures your project is completed on time, within budget, and exceeds your expectations.

1

Discover

We listen to your vision, assess your space, and understand your needs and budget to create the perfect plan.

2

Design

Our team creates detailed plans and 3D renderings, selecting materials and finishes that match your style.

3

Build

Expert craftsmen bring your vision to life with meticulous attention to detail and regular progress updates.

4

Enjoy

Walk through your completed project, receive your warranty documentation, and start enjoying your new space!
