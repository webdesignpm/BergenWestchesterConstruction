About Bergen Westchester

Three Decades of Construction Excellence

Founded in 1990 by Michael Venezia, Bergen Westchester Construction LLC has been transforming homes and commercial spaces across New Jersey and New York for over three decades. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted choice for discerning property owners.

We pride ourselves on delivering projects on time, within budget, and exceeding expectations. Every project comes with our comprehensive 5-year warranty and satisfaction guarantee.

Our Process

From Vision to Reality

1

Discover

We listen to your vision, assess your space, and understand your needs and budget to create the perfect plan.

2
